Coming soon: Mail Art show

Knights Templar Oasis is holding an art show for Mail Art. We're defining Mail Art as any artistic work that can be sent through the mail on its own, without needing to be put in an envelope, box, etc. Think postcards, but anything that makes it to us through standard mail will be accepted. Any art form (drawing, painting, digital prints, etc) is ok.

Submission window will run from January to March, with the opening happening at the spring equinox.

Gnostic Mass

A public celebration of Liber XV: The Gnostic Mass, the central public and private ritual of the Ordo Templi Orientis, through the auspices of Ecclesia Gnostica Catholica. The Gnostic Mass is a beautiful ritual that celebrates the mysteries of Thelema and our Order. It is a magical rite that exemplifies the union of the individual soul with the Absolute, culminating in a Eucharistic sacrament embodying Life and Joy.

Join us - every 2nd Sunday of the month.
