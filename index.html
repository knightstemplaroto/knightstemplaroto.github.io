Gnostic Mass

A public celebration of Liber XV: The Gnostic Mass, the central public and private ritual of the Ordo Templi Orientis, through the auspices of Ecclesia Gnostica Catholica. The Gnostic Mass is a beautiful ritual that celebrates the mysteries of Thelema and our Order. It is a magical rite that exemplifies the union of the individual soul with the Absolute, culminating in a Eucharistic sacrament embodying Life and Joy.

Every 2nd and 4th Sunday of the month.
