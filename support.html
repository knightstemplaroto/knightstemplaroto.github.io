Support Knights Templar Oasis

The operation of our oasis is funded by the dues and pledges of our members. Donations and dues payment can be made in person by cash or check to any oasis officer, who will log your payment and issue you a receipt.

Payments may also be made by PayPal through this website, below.

To make a one time donation of any amount, click on the donate button.

To set up a recurring monthly or weekly payment through PayPal, please choose one of the preset contribution levels and click on the Subscribe button. You may cancel the subscription using the Unsubscribe button, or by logging into your PayPal account and following their instructions.

Please direct any questions that you may have to the master of the oasis or the treasurer.
